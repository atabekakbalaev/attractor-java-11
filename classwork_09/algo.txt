Steps to fry potatoes:
1. Get all the ingredients
2. Rinse all potatoes
3. Dry them
4. Peel them
5. Cut potatoes in strips with knife
6. Pour oil in a frying pan till the halway of the sides of the pan
7. Dry the potatoes with a towel paper
8. Until all potatoes are fried:
    - Fry the potatoes in batches
    - Remove the potatoes from the pan into a bowl
10. Add salt to taste
11. Serve
